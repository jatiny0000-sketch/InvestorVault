Questions

Good questions, honest answers

Do I have to pay before seeing the data?

No. We send a free verified sample and the exact record count for your filters first. You only pay once you're happy.

How fresh and accurate is the data?

Databases are refreshed monthly, deduplicated, and manually spot-checked. We target 98%+ deliverability and replace dead records within the agreed threshold.

Is this compliant?

We source in line with DPDP 2023 and GDPR principles, honour opt-out requests, and recommend lawful, consent-based outreach. You're responsible for compliant usage in your campaigns.

What format do I receive?

A clean, ready-to-use Excel (.xlsx) or CSV file, structured with the fields relevant to your category.

Can you build a custom list?

Yes. Choose "Custom / Other" in the form and describe your exact target — we'll scope it and quote you.
